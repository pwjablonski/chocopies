How it works

As part of the Asia Society Triennial, the virtual “Eat Chocopie Together” art initiative seeks to further extend love, compassion, and humanity to promote peace on earth. Conceived in response to the COVID-19 pandemic, the project offers virtual Chocopies to be shared digitally by anyone, to send to a friend as a warm hello in this time of social distancing, symbolizing a gesture towards friendship. Furthermore, each share raises funds for The Korean American Community Foundation’s COVID-19 Action Fund, a charity set up to aid the Korean-American community affected by the pandemic.

Here’s how it Works Step-by-Step:

  • The homepage displays a map of the Korean peninsula, and a tally of Chocopies shared to date. Click anywhere on the map to activate a Zoomed-in map.
  • The Zoomed-in map is made of individual Chocopies of five different design available to be shared: LOVE, UNITY, PEACE, SHARE, EAT. Previously shared Choco Pies are replaced with the sharer’s name. Click and drag around to see more of the map.
  • Click on any Choco Pie to activate the sharing process.
  • Enter the Sender’s name, and email address, followed by the recipient’s name and email address you would be sending the Choco Pie to. You may add a personal message. Hit Send when ready.
  • If everything checks out, the Choco Pie will be virtually shared! Your recipient friend will receive it in the form of an email to the inbox you have specified.
  • The recipient friend has the option to click to “eat” the virtual Choco Pie, a symbolic gesture to reciprocate.
  • Both sharer’s and recipient’s names are acknowledged on the Korean Choco Pies Map.

Charity to Korean Americans Community Foundation

COVID-19 Action Fund By participating in “Eat Chocopie Together,” you are helping to raise funds towards the Korean American Community Foundation’s COVID-19 Action Fund in support of non-profit communities affected by the Coronavirus pandemic.

One complete share raises a total of US$2, when:

  • One Chocopie is shared with one friend = US$1 raised in sender’s name.
  • The shared Chocopie is ‘eaten’ by a recipient = US$1 raised in recipient’s name.

US$5,000 has already been generously pledged by [XXXXX]. The project comes to an end when the target of all 2,500 Choco Pies are shared and the pledged target of US$5,000 is raised, or by June 27, 2021 where the amount raised by that date will be donated to charity.

Note: Users do not, at any time, contribute any money of their own. The participation of sharing and eating Choco Pies together raises the donation to the charity fund.
